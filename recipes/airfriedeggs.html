Vego Lasagna

Description

Tasty and Easy alternative to the original. This is a vegetarian recepie. The use of cottage cheese makes it sliglty simpler than a regular lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 10 ounce frozen chopped spinach
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • 32 ounce crushed tomatoes
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 2 cups cottage cheese
  • 8 ounce mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • ½ cup chopped fresh parsley
  • ¼ cup parmesan cheese, garted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 uncooked lasagna noodles

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  3. Add spinach, onion, garlic, oregano, and basil; sauté until onion has softened, 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in spaghetti sauce and water; simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, mix cottage cheese, mozzarella, parsley, Parmesan, egg, salt, and pepper together in a bowl - until well combined.
  6. Remove sauce from the heat; spoon a small amount into the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  7. Place four uncooked noodles on top of sauce, then top with 1/3 of the remaining sauce. Layer with four more noodles, 1/2 of the remaining sauce, and 1/2 of the cheese mixture. Repeat layers once more with remaining noodles, sauce, and cheese mixture.
  8. Cover with foil.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 55 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for 15 more minutes. (Let sit for 10 minutes before serving.)
