Air Fried Eggs

Description

As esasy as they are tasty! This quick and easy dish is just as good when you want a healthy snack as when you want something differnt for dinner.

Takes less than 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg (/serving)
  • grounded salt and black pepper
  • cooking spray
  • 1 piece aluminum foil (4x5 inches)
  • chopped chive

Instructions

  1. Preheat an air fyre to 380 degrees F (193 degrees C).
  2. Shape foil piece into a circular shape, leaving the bottom flat to form a bowl, and set in the air fryer basket.
  3. Spray the bottom of the bowl with non-stick cooking spray.
  4. Crack egg into the center of the foil and season with salt and pepper
  5. Cook for 4 minutes.
  6. Remove foil bowl from the basket and garnish with chopped chive.
