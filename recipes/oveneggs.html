Oven Eggs

Description

Quick and esye! Makes for a good snack or something to treat your guests to!

Takes: about 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 12 eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • ¼ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Beat eggs, milk, and salt in a bowl until blended.
  3. Stir in green onions.
  4. Spray a muffin tray (or 12 mini baking dishes).
  5. Bake in oven for about 25 minutes, until set.
  6. Sprinkle cheddar and bake for another 2-3 minutes.
