Fried Rice

Description

Quick and Cozy meal! Perfect for the fall and winter when u want a qucik but warm meal to fill you up!

Takes: about 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 12 ounce firm tofu
  • 2 carrots (peeled)
  • 2 stalks celery
  • Â¼ onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ground salt & black pepper
  • 3 cups cooked white rice
  • 4 eggs
  • 8 ounce frozen chopped spinach
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha hot sauce

Instructions

  1. Dice tofu, carrots, celery and onion
  2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add tofu, carrots, celery, onion, and garlic; cook and stir until vegetables are just soft (5 to 10 minutes).
  4. Stir rice, egg, and spinach into tofu mixture until egg is no longer runny (ca. 5 minutes).
  5. Stir in Sriracha sauce; season with salt and pepper.
